THE LORE

The tale of the Curse Bound focuses on two mercenary best friends, known as : Star and Skat, a Warrior and a Mage.
These fighters with troubled pasts filled with pain, loss and corruption will rise up to change thenselves and this corrupt world they are in.

STAR : The son of a failed mercenary born with mana deficiency, making him unable to cast spells or certain skills is a subject of the neglect of the kingdom. This young Warrior aims for absolute strength and wishes to overcome his setback at any cost.

SKAT ( Shanara ) : She's the descendent of the now forgotten Vampire Kingdom's Imperial Family
( known as the Fangs Tribute ), and of the Moonlight Family ( the current Imperial Family ).
She was raised by a witch, and is determined to protect the ones close to her and to refine her powers.

In a world where anyone can have weird powers and skills, some are unable or develop defiencies making them lowlives in the eyes of the society, foring the to hide and become slaves. But worse than that, the existences of hybrides ine the Human Empire is considered worse and may bring death on the parents of the product itself unless seen as useful, a rule placed by the Imperial Family..
The Imperial Family are the one ruling over the now Human Kingdom after the blood event, these royals despises miwed bloods and will kill and remove every remnant of hybrid they get their hands on unless one comes as talented or useful to the kingdom.
Thought they rule the Human Empire, their deeds shows teh treats of a Demon.
